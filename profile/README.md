# Heptabase – Visual Knowledge Management & Whiteboard Tool

**Heptabase** is a powerful, flexible knowledge management solution designed to help individuals and teams organize research, visualize connections, and achieve deeper understanding.  
With **Heptabase**, you can transform chaotic notes into structured whiteboard maps.  
**Heptabase** supports card-based organization, backlinks, and cross-device synchronization, making it an essential thinking companion for researchers, students, and creators.

**Heptabase** combines the simplicity of note-taking with the power of spatial thinking. Unlike basic note tools, **Heptabase** offers deep customization: whiteboard canvases, nested cards, tags, and bidirectional links.  
**Heptabase** works seamlessly with your existing workflow and provides real-time updates across all devices.  
Whether you manage literature reviews or creative projects, **Heptabase** adapts to your thinking, not the other way around.

With **Heptabase**, you gain clarity, reduce information overload, and connect more ideas every day.

## Key Features of Heptabase

- **Whiteboard Canvas** – **Heptabase** lets you arrange cards spatially on infinite boards.  
- **Card System** – **Heptabase** treats every note as a rich, editable card.  
- **Bidirectional Links** – **Heptabase** automatically shows connections between cards.  
- **Tag Hierarchy** – Create nested tags inside **Heptabase** for deep organization.  
- **Knowledge Graph** – **Heptabase** visualizes your entire network of ideas.  
- **Real-time Collaboration** – Share whiteboards and edit together in **Heptabase**.  
- **Backup & Export** – Export your **Heptabase** data to Markdown, JSON, or PDF.  
- **Cross-Platform** – Use **Heptabase** on iOS, Android, Linux, and web.  
- **Dark Mode** – Use **Heptabase** with system-wide dark theme.  
- **Search & Replace** – Find any card instantly using **Heptabase** advanced search.  
- **Templates** – Save whiteboard structures inside **Heptabase** for reuse.  
- **Offline Mode** – **Heptabase** works offline and syncs when connection returns.  
- **Push Notifications** – Get reminders from **Heptabase** for shared boards.  
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Navigate **Heptabase** faster with hotkeys.  
- **Attachment Support** – Add files, images, and PDFs inside **Heptabase**.
